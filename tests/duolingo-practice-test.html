ABOUT DUOLINGO ENGLISH TEST

The Duolingo English Test (DET) is a modern, accessible English proficiency assessment designed for non-native English speakers seeking university admission or professional opportunities. It offers a quick, affordable, and convenient alternative to traditional standardized tests.

Key Features

The DET can be taken from home using a webcam and microphone. Results are typically available within 2 days, making it ideal for students with tight application deadlines. The test covers reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills through adaptive questioning.

Acceptance

Over 2,500 universities and institutions worldwide accept Duolingo English Test scores, including many prestigious universities in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does the Duolingo English Test cost?

The Duolingo English Test costs $49 USD, which is significantly cheaper than TOEFL ($245) or IELTS (¬£161+). Multiple attempts are allowed at this price.

How long is the Duolingo English Test?

The test takes approximately 1 hour to complete, including instructions and verification. It's much shorter than TOEFL (3 hours) and IELTS (2 hours 45 minutes).

Where can I take the Duolingo English Test?

You can take the DET from anywhere with a computer, webcam, and internet connection. Proctoring is done online via AI and human reviewers.

What is a good Duolingo English Test score?

A score of 100-110 is generally considered good for university admission. Top universities may require 110-120+. Check specific institution requirements.

How is the Duolingo English Test scored?

The DET uses AI-powered adaptive testing that adjusts question difficulty based on your performance. Scores range from 10-160 and reflect reading, writing, speaking, and listening abilities.
