ABOUT PTE ACADEMIC

The Pearson Test of English (PTE) Academic is a computer-delivered English proficiency test developed by Pearson. It is widely accepted by universities and professional organizations worldwide for assessing the English language skills of non-native speakers.

Key Advantages

PTE Academic offers quick turnaround times (results in 5 business days), flexible test scheduling, and AI-powered scoring that provides objective assessment. It is particularly popular among students applying to universities in Australia, Canada, and increasingly in the UK and USA.

Scoring System

PTE Academic scores range from 10-90, with scores reported in 10-point increments. Each skill (Speaking, Writing, Reading, Listening) is scored from 10-90, and an overall score is calculated.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the difference between PTE and other English tests?

PTE is fully computer-based with AI scoring, has flexible test dates, faster results, and is particularly strong in Australia. It is also generally cheaper than IELTS and TOEFL and has more test centers globally.

How long is PTE Academic?

The test takes exactly 2 hours from start to finish. This includes all sections: Speaking & Writing, Reading, and Listening.

What is a good PTE score?

A score of 60-65 is generally considered good for most university programs. Top universities may require 70+. Most professional organizations accept scores of 70 and above.

How often can I retake PTE?

You can take PTE as many times as you want. Test centers typically offer multiple dates per week, making it easy to retake if needed.

Do universities accept PTE instead of IELTS?

Yes, over 3,000 institutions worldwide accept PTE Academic, including major universities in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia. Check with your target university for their specific requirements.
